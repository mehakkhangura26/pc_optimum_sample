Early Alzheimer's Signs
Know the difference between normal aging and warning signs
Normal Aging
Making a bad decision once in a while
Alzheimer's Warning
Poor judgment and decision-making consistently
Missing a monthly payment occasionally
Problems managing finances or paying bills
Forgetting which day it is and remembering it later
Losing track of dates, seasons, and passage of time
Sometimes forgetting which word to use
Trouble having a conversation or finding the right words
Losing things from time to time
Misplacing items and unable to retrace steps to find them
Needing help to use settings on a microwave
Difficulty completing familiar tasks at home or work
Vision changes related to cataracts
New problems with words, vision, or spatial relationships
Sometimes feeling weary of work or social obligations
Withdrawal from work projects or social activities
